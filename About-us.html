About Us

Skilled Medical is an international medical recruitment, placement and services firm based in London and Melbourne. Established in 2005, we specialise in placing health professionals in locum, temporary and permanent positions across the United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand.We source qualified medical personnel from local and global markets and provide staffing solutions to public and private health services, general practices and the corporate sector. As a doctor-led firm, our medical workforce knowledge helps to ensure our placements are well matched to the needs of our clients and their communities.
